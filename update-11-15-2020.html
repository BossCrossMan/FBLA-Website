FBLA and FBLA-ML Members and Advisers,
 
In a year where everything is different and nothing is surprising, FBLA-PBL members are aspiring to explore, experience, and engage like never before. We are rounding out our first quarter of the membership year and have seen our future business leader members accomplish so much! Last month we saw registrations for the Stock Market Game increase by 200%, we had many new students begin their BAA journey, and we networked with our national officers virtually during our kick-off region meetings. We also hit over 10,000 followers on Instagram allowing us to enhance our engagement with new features and greater reach.
 
I am so proud to be the National President during a membership year that is highlighted by resiliency such as this. It is crucial though that members are all registered and accounted for before the end of 2020 to ensure that all members are recognized for their outstanding achievements. Here are some reminders for advisers and members to help grow their chapters!
 
Membership
Members: Now is the time to register and pay for member dues.  Are you registered? If not, contact your adviser to register or email kpoma@fbla.org and she can get you registered and paid so that you don’t miss out on the following opportunities.  Members receive a digital membership card, can map out their career path with Tallo, attend the KPMG Branding U Series, and participate in our Business Achievement Awards (FBLA) and LEAD (FBLA-ML) and earn lapel pins and digital badges. I challenge you to be registered and paid by December 1!
 
Non-Stop November
The second portion of the FBLA National Chapter Challenge, Non-Stop November, is taking place throughout this month. Make sure to take part to get your chapter recognized! All chapters participating in Non-Stop November will receive digital swag and a code to a 10 percent MarketPlace Discount.  All submissions will be judged on Activity 1 - Prepare a 30 second recruitment video and a one-page infographic about FBLA.  First - Tenth Place will be awarded with chapters receiving plaques and a FBLA recruitment/celebration kit worth more than $1,000.  Chapters do not have to have completed Super Sweeps to do Non-Stop November. 
  
Prematurity Awareness Month
This month is Prematurity Awareness Month!
 
Why does this matter?
November is the most important time to increase awareness for our main fundraising partner, the March of Dimes. We understand that donations to their cause may be unfeasible right now for some people and chapters, but raising awareness for their cause is extremely important over the next few weeks.
 
Stay tuned to our national social media accounts for important posts regarding their cause to help end preterm birth!
 
Leadership Community
Advisers: Make sure to stay updated on important FBLA-related information and collaborate with your fellow advisers across the nation with our Leadership Communities! It is a great way to connect with our national team and get access to new resources that can be shared with your members.
 
FBLA Big 10
Congrats to Alpharetta and Lambert High School for being tied in first place in our Big 10 program thus far this year. We are astounded by the effort chapters are taking to get members this year regardless of the current environment in our country.
 
Again, if you ever need anything, you know how to reach us. We are here for you!
